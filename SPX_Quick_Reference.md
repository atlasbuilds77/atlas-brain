# SPX Day Trading Quick Reference

## KEY TIMES (EST)
- **9:30-10:30 AM**: Opening range, highest volatility
- **10:30 AM**: Common reversal time
- **3:00-4:00 PM**: Power hour, institutional rebalancing
- **80% of gaps fill by noon**

## SUPPORT/RESISTANCE IDENTIFICATION
1. **Pivot Points** (Previous day H/L/C)
2. **Gamma Levels** (Call/Put walls via options data)
3. **Previous Day Levels** (High/Low/Close)
4. **Round Numbers** (Psychological levels)
5. **Moving Averages** (20/50/200 day)

## INTRADAY PATTERNS
### Opening Range Breakout (ORB)
- First 15-30 min establishes range
- 64% upside breakouts lead to green closes
- Stop: 75% retracement of opening range

### Gap Fills
- Overall: ~60% fill rate in uptrends
- Small gaps (0-0.19%): 87-92% fill rate
- Medium gaps (0.2-0.39%): 53-69% fill rate
- Large gaps (>0.4%): <50% fill rate

### Day-by-Day Gap Fill Stats:

- **Tuesday**: Gap down 55%, Gap up 67%
- **Wednesday**: Gap down 80%, Gap up 62%
- **Thursday**: Gap down 60%, Gap up 57%
- **Friday**: Gap down 20%, Gap up 64%

## VWAP STRATEGIES
- **Above VWAP**: Bullish bias
- **Below VWAP**: Bearish bias
- **Virgin VWAP**: First touch from above/below
- **Anchored VWAP**: From significant events

### Trading Setups:
1. VWAP pullback with trend
2. VWAP rejection fade
3. VWAP break with volume

## VIX RELATIONSHIP
- **80% inverse correlation** with SPX
- **VIX < 15**: Low volatility, complacency
- **VIX 15-25**: Normal range
- **VIX > 25**: Elevated fear
- **VIX > 40**: Extreme fear, potential reversal

### Expected Moves:
- Daily: VIX / √365 (e.g., VIX 20 = ~1.05%)
- Weekly: VIX / √52

## GAMMA & DEALER POSITIONING
- **Call Wall**: Resistance (holds 83% of sessions)
- **Put Wall**: Support
- **Positive Gamma**: Dealers stabilize (range-bound)
- **Negative Gamma**: Dealers amplify (trending)

### Gamma Squeeze Setup:
1. Identify gamma levels (SpotGamma)
2. Watch for falling VIX + rising SPX
3. Break through gamma level with falling vol
4. Manage risk - squeezes can reverse quickly

## ORDER FLOW CLUES
### Institutional Activity:
- **Large prints** (>100 contracts)
- **Absorption** at key levels
- **Sequence** of prints at bid/ask
- **Options flow** spikes

### Tape Reading:
- Follow large prints
- Watch for absorption
- Monitor bid/ask size changes

## MOVING AVERAGES
- **20-day**: Short-term trend
- **50-day**: Intermediate trend
- **200-day**: Long-term trend
- **Price > All MAs**: Bullish
- **Price < All MAs**: Bearish

## NEWS/MACRO EVENTS
### Key Times:
- **8:30 AM**: Economic data (CPI, Jobs, GDP)
- **2:00 PM**: FOMC decisions

### Trading Approach:
1. Reduce size before events
2. Wait for initial reaction
3. Trade the range (often mean-reverts)
4. Follow institutional flow

## RISK MANAGEMENT
- **1-2% risk per trade**
- **Stop losses** at structure breaks
- **Trail stops** in trends
- **Reduce size** during high VIX
