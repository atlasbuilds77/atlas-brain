# Humanoid Robot Parts Sourcing Guide

## Where to Buy Components

### 1. Servo Motors (Most Critical Purchase)

**Budget Options (China Direct):**
- **AliExpress:** Best prices, 2-4 week shipping
  - Search: "MG996R metal gear servo"
  - Look for sellers with 95%+ rating, 1000+ orders
  - Expect to pay $6-8 each in bulk (10+ pieces)
- **Banggood:** Similar to AliExpress
  - Often faster shipping options
  - Better customer service for returns

**Mid-Range (US/EU Warehouses):**
- **Amazon:** Faster shipping, higher prices
  - MG996R: $8-12 each
  - Good for small quantities or urgent needs
  - Check reviews carefully
- **RobotShop:** Robotics specialty
  - Higher quality assurance
  - Better technical support
  - Prices 20-30% higher than China

**Premium (For Critical Joints):**
- **ServoCity:** Professional-grade servos
  - Higher torque, better precision
  - 2-3x the cost of MG996R
  - Only for load-bearing joints if budget allows

### 2. Electronics & Controllers

**Microcontrollers:**
- **Arduino Mega 2560:**
  - AliExpress: $12-15 (clone)
  - Amazon: $35-45 (official)
  - eBay: $15-25 (used/refurbished)
- **Arduino Nano:**
  - $3-5 each from China
  - Good for distributed control

**Servo Controllers:**
- **16-channel PWM shield:**
  - AliExpress: $8-12
  - Amazon: $15-20
  - Key feature: I2C interface

**Raspberry Pi:**
- **Pi Zero 2W:** $15-20 (when in stock)
- **Pi 3A+/4:** $35-75
- **Check:** rpilocator.com for stock alerts

### 3. Power System Components

**Batteries:**
- **3S LiPo 2200mAh:**
  - HobbyKing: $15-25
  - Amazon: $20-30
  - Local RC hobby shops: $25-35
- **Chargers:**
  - IMAX B6 clone: $15-25
  - Genuine SkyRC: $40-60

**Voltage Regulators:**
- **Buck converters (LM2596):**
  - 10-pack on AliExpress: $8-12
  - Adjustable 3-35V, 3A output
- **UBEC (5V/6V):**
  - $3-5 each
  - For servo power regulation

### 4. Structural Materials

**3D Printing Filament:**
- **PLA+ (1kg spool):**
  - eSUN, SUNLU, Overture: $20-25
  - Amazon Prime: Fast delivery
  - MatterHackers: Premium quality
- **PETG (for higher temp):**
  - $25-30 per kg
  - Better for parts near motors

**Laser Cutting Materials:**
- **3mm acrylic:**
  - Ponoko, SendCutSend: Custom cutting
  - Local makerspaces: Often have stock
  - Online plastics suppliers

**Hardware:**
- **M3 screw assortment:**
  - Amazon: $10-15 for 500+ pieces
  - Includes nuts, washers, spacers
- **Servo horns/extensions:**
  - AliExpress: $5 for 50-piece set
  - Various types and sizes

### 5. Tools & Equipment

**Essential Tools:**
- **Soldering iron:** $20-40 (TS100 recommended)
- **Multimeter:** $20-50
- **Screwdrivers:** Precision set $10-20
- **Wire cutters/strippers:** $10-15

**Optional but Helpful:**
- **3D printer:** Ender 3 ($200-250)
- **Oscilloscope:** $50-100 (DSO150)
- **Power supply:** Adjustable bench supply ($50-100)

## Shipping Considerations

**From China:**
- **ePacket:** 2-4 weeks, often free over $20
- **AliExpress Standard:** 3-6 weeks, tracked
- **DHL/FedEx:** 1-2 weeks, $20-40 shipping

**Domestic (US):**
- **Amazon Prime:** 1-2 days
- **Newegg:** 3-5 days
- **Specialty stores:** 5-10 days

## Timing Your Purchases

**Phase 1 (Planning):**
- Order from China (4-6 week lead time)
- Servos, electronics, hardware

**Phase 2 (Fabrication):**
- Domestic sources for urgent items
- Filament, tools, consumables

**Phase 3 (Assembly):**
- Local stores for last-minute needs
- Replacement parts, additional hardware

## Quality vs Cost Trade-offs

**Worth Spending More:**
1. **Load-bearing servos** - avoid cheap clones
2. **Batteries** - safety critical
3. **Power connectors** - prevent fires
4. **Filament for structural parts** - avoid warping

**Can Save Money:**
1. **Non-critical electronics** (sensors, LEDs)
2. **Hardware** (screws, nuts, washers)
3. **Prototyping materials** (test prints)
4. **Cables and connectors** (within reason)

## Bulk Buying Strategy

**For Group/Makerspace Builds:**
- **Servos:** Order 50+ for best pricing
- **Electronics:** Buy microcontroller lots
- **Hardware:** Get assortment kits
- **Filament:** 5+ spools for volume discount

**Minimum Order Quantities:**
- AliExpress: Often 1-2 pieces minimum
- Professional suppliers: $50-100 minimum order
- Wholesale: 10+ pieces for discount

## Avoiding Counterfeits

**Red Flags:**
- Prices significantly below market
- No brand markings on components
- Poor packaging
- Seller with low feedback

**Verification Tips:**
- Buy from official stores on platforms
- Check for authentication stickers
- Test components upon arrival
- Keep records for returns

## Return Policies

**China Suppliers:**
- 15-30 day return window
- Buyer often pays return shipping
- Dispute resolution through platform

**Domestic Suppliers:**
- 30-90 day returns
- Often free returns
- Better customer service

## Timing Your Build

**Option A: Fast Build (4 weeks)**
- Buy everything domestically
- Higher cost, faster delivery
- Good for time-sensitive projects

**Option B: Balanced (8 weeks)**
- Critical parts domestically
- Non-critical from China
- Best value for money

**Option C: Minimum Cost (12+ weeks)**
- Everything from China
- Wait for sales/shipping
- Maximum savings
