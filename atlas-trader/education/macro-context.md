   - Gold-to-silver ratio behavior key indicator

2. 1987 Black Monday
   - October crash, market fell 22% in one day
   - "Panic season" = Aug-Oct historically volatile
   - Low summer liquidity + fear = cascade

3. Current Parallels (2026)

   - Economists warning of patterns
