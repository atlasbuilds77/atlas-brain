## Key Insights for Orion

### 1. **Skill Development Progression**
The 100 days are strategically sequenced:
- **Days 1-30**: Foundation (components, typography, color)
- **Days 31-60**: Interaction (user flows, micro-interactions)
- **Days 61-90**: Systems (design systems, accessibility)
- **Days 91-100**: Portfolio (case studies, presentation)

### 2. **Common Success Patterns**
- **Visual Consistency**: Cohesive color palettes, typography scales, spacing systems
- **Clear Hierarchy**: Obvious primary actions, effective use of whitespace
- **User-Centered Solutions**: Intuitive navigation, clear feedback mechanisms
- **Creative Interpretation**: Unique brand personalities, thematic consistency

### 3. **How Atlas Can Help**
- **Prompt Interpretation**: Brainstorming concepts and themes
- **Design System Support**: Maintaining consistency across days
- **Workflow Optimization**: Daily routines and time management
- **Feedback & Iteration**: Objective critiques and improvement suggestions
- **Portfolio Development**: Case study creation and presentation guidance
