# Hyperliquid Alpha Research - Key Findings

## Completed Research Tasks:

### 1. Order Book Differences vs CEXs:
- **Fully on-chain CLOB** with transparent state (unlike CEX off-chain matching)
- **No liquidation clearance fees** (CEXs charge fees)
- **Mark price for liquidations** = CEX prices + book state (more robust)
- **Professional trader dominance** = faster edge erosion for retail

### 2. Working vs Failing Patterns:

**✅ WORKING:**
- Low-leverage trend following (2x-5x)
- Funding-aware position trading (opposite extreme funding)
- Range trading BTC/ETH/SOL (high liquidity)
- Session-based trading (US hours only)

**❌ FAILING:**
- High-leverage scalping (20x-50x) - professionals dominate
- Blind whale copy-trading - edge disappears
- Overtrading low-liquidity pairs - slippage kills
- Emotional trading post-liquidation - account wipe

### 3. Best Liquidity Pairs:
- **Tier 1**: BTC/USDC, ETH/USDC, SOL/USDC (tight spreads)
- **Tier 2**: HYPE/USDC, major blue-chip alts
- **Avoid**: Low-volume memes, <$1M daily volume pairs

### 4. Funding Rate Patterns:
- **4% hourly cap** (higher than CEXs) - creates extreme opportunities
- **Hourly payments** (not 8-hour)
- **Edge**: Trade opposite extreme funding (Hyperliquid users = perma-bullish)
- **Arbitrage**: Monitor vs Binance/Bybit/dYdX for differences

### 5. Known Edges:
- **Liquidation flow competition** (no internalization, no fees)
- **HLP liquidator vault** - community profits from backstop liquidations
- **Transparent risk formulas** - precise calculation possible
- **Funding extremes** often precede reversals

### 6. Community Strategies:
- **Risk-first mindset**: 0.5%-1% per trade, small losses accepted
- **Funding farming**: Short during extreme positive funding (1x-3x)
- **HLP staking + trading** combo
- **Tools**: Hyperdash (whale tracking), FundingView (arbitrage), CoinGlass (heatmaps)

## Actionable Insights:

### For Scalping:
1. **Only high-liquidity pairs** (BTC/ETH/SOL)
2. **Avoid >10x leverage** - professionals dominate short-term
3. **Session timing critical** - US market hours only
4. **Monitor funding** - avoid paying excessive rates

### For Alpha:
1. **Funding arbitrage**: Cross-venue opportunities exist
2. **Liquidation competition**: Can profit from volatility
3. **HLP exposure**: Earn liquidation profits via staking
4. **Whale activity as context** (not signals)

### Key Tools Recommended:
- Hyperliquid app (built-in funding comparison)
- Coinalyze/CoinGlass (funding history, liquidation maps)
- Hyperdash (real-time whale tracking)
- FundingView (cross-exchange arbitrage)

## Critical Risk Notes:
- **4% hourly funding** can destroy capital if wrong side
- **Professional competition** = faster edge erosion
- **On-chain transparency** = strategies exposed
- **Liquidation mechanics** equally brutal despite transparency

## Bottom Line:
Hyperliquid rewards **discipline, funding awareness, and high-liquidity focus**. Punishes **overleverage, emotional trading, and low-liquidity chasing**. Most consistent edge = **funding rate mean reversion + risk management**.
