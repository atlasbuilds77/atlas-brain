# Quick Guide: Leverage Trading for Small Accounts on Solana

## TL;DR - Most Important Rules

### 🚨 NEVER DO (Account Blow-up Guaranteed)
1. ❌ Use >20x leverage with <$200 account
2. ❌ Trade without stop losses
3. ❌ Risk >5% of account on any trade
4. ❌ Add to losing positions
5. ❌ Trade emotionally after losses

### ✅ ALWAYS DO (Survival & Profit)
1. ✅ Max 5x leverage for beginners
2. ✅ Risk 1-2% per trade maximum
3. ✅ Set stop loss immediately after entry
4. ✅ Use isolated margin when available
5. ✅ Keep 20%+ buffer from liquidation price

## 🎯 Position Sizing Formula

**For $100 account:**
```
Position Size = ($100 × Risk%) ÷ Stop Distance
```

**Example:**
- Risk: 2% ($2)
- Stop: 5% away
- Position = ($100 × 0.02) ÷ 0.05 = $40
- With 5x leverage = $200 controlled position

## 🔄 Funding Rate Arbitrage (Simple)

### When to Enter:
- **Go LONG** when funding rate is NEGATIVE (shorts pay longs)
- **Go SHORT** when funding rate is POSITIVE (longs pay shorts)

### Minimum Rates to Act:
- >0.1% hourly on Drift
- Monitor: docs.drift.trade/trading/funding-rates

## 🆘 Emergency Rules

### If losing 20% of account:
- STOP TRADING for 1 week
- Review all trades
- Reduce leverage by 50%
- Reduce position size by 50%

### If losing 50% of account:
- STOP TRADING for 1 month
- Go back to demo trading
- Re-learn basics
- Consider if leverage trading is for you

## 📈 Profit Taking Rules

1. **Take 50% profit** at 2x risk (2R)
2. **Move stop to breakeven** at 1.5x risk (1.5R)
3. **Let remainder run** with trailing stop
4. **Withdraw profits** monthly to secure gains
