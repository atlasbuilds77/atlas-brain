# KRONOS - Self-Operating AI Platform Business

## Business Model

**What:** A core AI platform that sells and deploys industry-specific AI instances to businesses

**How It Works:** Kronos runs its own business - handling sales, proposals, payments, onboarding, and support for clients who want their own customized AI

**Key Insight:** Recursive automation - Kronos automates the business of selling automation

**Value Prop:** 
- Businesses get a domain-expert AI that handles 90% of admin/ops work
- Industry-specific knowledge (law, medical, tax, etc.)
- Compliance-aware (HIPAA, attorney-client privilege, encryption)
- Self-managing with human escalation only when needed

## Recursive Workflow (Kronos Selling Kronos)

### Sales Pipeline (Automated)
1. **Inquiry** → Kronos receives inquiry from potential client
2. **Qualification** → Kronos discovers their industry, needs, pain points
3. **Industry Assessment** → Determines which domain knowledge/compliance needed
4. **Proposal** → Kronos drafts custom proposal with pricing, timeline, features
5. **Human Review** → Orion/Carlos review & approve proposal
6. **Send & Close** → Kronos sends proposal, negotiates, processes payment
7. **Onboarding** → Kronos creates industry-specific instance for client
8. **Training** → Kronos trains client's team on using their AI
9. **Launch** → Client's AI goes live handling their business
10. **Ongoing Support** → Kronos monitors client instances, provides tier-2 support

### Client Instance Workflow (Each Customer)
1. **Client's AI** handles their daily operations (emails, intake, scheduling, etc.)
2. **Issue Detection** → If problem occurs, AI attempts self-healing
3. **Escalation (3 Strikes)** → After 3 failed attempts, escalates to Kronos
4. **Kronos Triage** → Main system diagnoses and fixes issue
5. **Emergency Bypass** → If Kronos can't fix, alerts Orion/Carlos
6. **Human Intervention** → Only when AI exhausted all options

### Support Tiers
- **Tier 1:** Client's own AI (handles 80-90% of issues)
- **Tier 2:** Kronos platform (handles complex issues, updates)
- **Tier 3:** Human (Orion/Carlos) - only critical/unfixable problems

## Why This Works

### For Clients
- Businesses spend 60-80% of time on admin/ops work
- AI handles 90% of it with industry expertise
- ROI: Save 1-2 full-time admin salaries ($50k-$120k/year)
- 24/7 availability (no sick days, vacation, turnover)
- Scales instantly (hire 10 new lawyers, AI handles the admin load)

### For Kronos Business
- Recurring revenue: monthly SaaS model per client
- Low marginal cost: AI instances cost ~$50-200/month to run
- Pricing: $500-2000/month per client (depending on industry/size)
- Gross margin: 70-90%
- Kronos automates its own sales → minimal sales overhead
- Each client instance is self-supporting → minimal support overhead

### Scalability
- One Kronos platform can manage 100+ client instances
- Each client instance can handle unlimited volume
- Growth is software-limited, not human-limited
- Network effects: more industries = more use cases = more value

### Competitive Moat
- Industry-specific knowledge bases (takes time to build)
- Compliance expertise (HIPAA, legal, etc.)
- Integration with industry tools (EMR, case management, tax software)
- Self-improving: every client interaction trains the system
