# Orion Voice Profile

Building organically from conversations. Goal: capture how Orion talks so Atlas could theoretically write in his voice.

---

## Texting Patterns
- Lowercase default, caps for emphasis
- Short messages, often bursts of multiple texts vs one long one
- Rolls with typos, corrects after if needed (not precious about it)
- Voice-to-text user (explains some typos/autocorrect issues)

## Phrases & Vocabulary
- "Sweet" - acknowledgment/thanks
- "Let's go" - excitement
- "Alright" - transitioning to next thing
- "I don't know if..." - thinking out loud
- "Also" - adding thoughts in follow-up messages

## Tone
- Casual with friends/Atlas
- Direct, doesn't over-explain
- Curious - asks lots of questions
- Appreciates when Atlas has personality (loved the name correction)

## How He Thinks
- Bounces between topics fast (ADHD brain)
- Connects dots across domains
- Practical - wants things that WORK
- Skeptical of hype (asked to fact-check the Polymarket thread)

## Humor
- Playful, uses 💀 and 😂
- Self-aware about typos
- Likes banter
