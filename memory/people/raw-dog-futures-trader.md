## Entry Methodology

### 1. Entry Timing
- Based on **candle closes** (typically 1m candles)
- **Market orders** almost exclusively
- Very little spread/slippage with futures
- NO automation - discretionary trading where context matters

### 2. Entry Sequence (IFVG-based)

**Step 1:** Watch 1m candle approaching close  
**Step 2:** Candle closes through FVG → creates IFVG  
**Step 3:** Evaluate entry price vs IFVG  
**Step 4:** If close is too far from IFVG (bad R:R) → **wait for retrace** back into gap  
**Step 5:** **CRITICAL** - Don't limit order in the gap  
**Step 6:** **Wait for reaction** - Price retraces in → watch for "nice close out of it"  
**Step 7:** That close = confirmation price is respecting the IFVG  
**Step 8:** **THEN** market order on that confirming candle

**What this means:**
- Stacking confirmations (IFVG + retrace + reaction)
- Not just "price hit zone" but "price hit zone AND bounced"
- R:R filter prevents chasing
- Patience for better entry improves edge

**If it doesn't retrace back:**
- Just missed the move
- Continue looking for another setup
- Never chase

## Stop Loss Discipline

### Hard Stops (SMT-based entries)
- Set at the high/low where SMT was created
- Used for early entries based on Smart Money Techniques

### Soft Stops (FVG/IFVG-based entries)
- Close trade if candle closes above/below the FVG/IFVG
- Direction depends on long vs short
- **Always set hard stop ~10 points beyond soft stop** in case of fast price movement

## Key Concepts Shared

### FVG (Fair Value Gap)
- Price imbalance zone
- Area where price moved quickly leaving inefficiency

### IFVG (Inversion Fair Value Gap)
- Created when candle closes through FVG
- Becomes support/resistance zone
- Raw dog waits for reaction OUT of IFVG before entering

### SMT (Smart Money Technique)
- Used for directional bias
- Creates reference points for hard stops

### Confirmation Stacking
- Not just zone touch, but reaction
- Multiple confirmations required before entry:
  - IFVG created
  - Retrace into zone
  - Reaction (bounce) out of zone
  - Confirming candle close
