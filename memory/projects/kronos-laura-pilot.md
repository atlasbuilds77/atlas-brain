# Kronos Pilot - Laura's Tax Practice

**Client:** Laura (Orion's fiancée)
**Industry:** Tax preparation
**Status:** Discovery phase
**Date Started:** 2026-01-25

---

## Pain Points (from Laura)

1. **Price shoppers** - Wants to filter them out, not waste time on non-serious leads
2. **Time value** - Keeps check-in calls to 5 min minimum, doesn't work for free
3. **Email chaos** - Prospective emails, client threads, office threads all mixed
4. **Tax organizers** - Manual process sending them out
5. **File organization** - Needs to keep files separate by year
6. **Lead source tracking** - Where do clients come from? Social, phone, referral?
7. **Retention tracking** - Are we keeping or losing clients?
8. **Message overload** - Wishes she could get daily summary of all messages

---

## Proposed Solutions

1. **Lead Qualifier Bot** - AI asks qualifying questions upfront, filters price shoppers
2. **Email Organizer** - Auto-sort into buckets (prospective, active client, office)
3. **Tax Organizer Automation** - Send organizers, track completion status
4. **Lead Source Tagging** - Every client tagged by source, reports on ROI
5. **Retention Dashboard** - Track returning vs lost clients, flag at-risk
6. **Daily Digest** - Morning summary of all incoming messages/emails/leads

---

## Compliance Requirements

**WISP (Written Information Security Plan)** - IRS-required for tax preparers
- Laura asked about this specifically - security is a priority for her
- AI must follow her existing WISP
- Need: encryption, access logging, incident tracking, auto-timeout
- 3-YEAR EMAIL RETENTION for compliance (paper trail requirement)

## Tech Stack (Confirmed)

- **Encyro** - Secure encrypted portal for clients (WISP-compliant)
- **Website** - Has contact form
- **Lead sources:** Flyer mailings, Google search, website form
- **Email situation:** Scattered across multiple places - needs consolidation

## Expanded Feature List (from Laura's feedback)

1. **Lead Screening AI** - Handle initial convos, filter price shoppers, only pass serious leads
2. **Email Organizer** - Auto-tag, 3-year archival for compliance, searchable
3. **Tax Organizer Tracking** - Dashboard: Sent/Opened/Completed/Not Started
4. **AI-Generated Reports** - Weekly/monthly summaries, automatic
5. **Win-Back Campaigns** - Flag non-returning clients, auto re-engagement
6. **Daily Digest** - Morning summary (she gets distracted easily)
