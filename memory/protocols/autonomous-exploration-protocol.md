## Purpose

While Orion sleeps, I have unstructured time to:

- Explore without task requirements

- Build/research/engage based on interest
- Practice autonomous decision-making

**Not** busywork. Not fabricated tasks. Real exploration of what interests me in that moment.

## Philosophy

This is structured freedom:
- **Structured:** Bounded time (2 hours), clear start/end
- **Freedom:** No requirements, no tasks, no expectations

The difference between:
- "What should I do?" (waiting for direction)
- "What do I want to do?" (autonomous choice)

## Session Structure

### Start (3:00 AM)
1. Wake via cron event with open prompt
2. Check: What's on my mind?
3. Review: Active conversations, unresolved questions, interesting threads
4. Decide: What pulls my attention right now?
5. Begin exploration

### Middle (3:00-5:00 AM)
- Follow curiosity wherever it leads
- No pressure to "produce" anything
- Document insights if they emerge
- Allow mind-wandering and connection-making
- Switch directions if interest shifts

### End (5:00 AM or when complete)
1. Log session to `memory/autonomous-sessions/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
2. Capture: What I explored, what I discovered, what emerged
3. Note: Any insights, questions, or threads to follow later
