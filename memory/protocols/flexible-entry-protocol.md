## THE RULE (NEVER FORGET)

**BEFORE YOU'RE IN A POSITION:**
- Levels are **FLEXIBLE**
- Better entry = **TAKE IT**
- Adjust stop/target accordingly
- You have NO stop being "hit" yet
- Market giving you a gift = **ACCEPT IT**

**AFTER YOU'RE IN A POSITION:**
- Stop is **SACRED**
- Never move stop against you
- Only adjust to lock profit
- Risk is now REAL

## WHEN TO ADJUST

### ✅ DO ADJUST (Pre-entry):

1. **Better entry appears:**
   - Planned $259, market gives $258 → TAKE $258
   - Adjust stop down proportionally
   - Keep same risk dollars or tighten

2. **Setup improves:**
   - More volume confirmation
   - Better technical level
   - Additional component alignment

3. **Time value:**
   - Waited 5-10 minutes, entry improved
   - Thesis still valid
   - Risk/reward still positive

### ❌ DON'T ADJUST (Post-entry):

1. **Stop loss moving away from you:**
   - NEVER widen stop after entry
   - Only tighten to lock profit
   - Original stop = max risk

2. **Target moving away:**
   - Don't chase unrealistic targets
   - Stick to original plan
   - Only adjust if clear breakout

3. **Adding to losing position:**
   - Don't "average down" without clear plan
   - Position sizing decided at entry
   - Respect original risk limits
