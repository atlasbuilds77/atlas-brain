## WHY CHASING KILLS EDGE

1. **Destroys R:R** - Entry near target = no profit margin
2. **Emotional decision** - FOMO overrides logic
3. **Worse entry = bigger drawdown** - Same move, worse outcome
4. **Proves you didn't have conviction** - If you believed in $3.50, why enter at $4.80?

---

## THE RULE

### ✅ If you miss your entry:
- Move on
- Find next setup
- No emotion
- No regret

### ❌ NEVER do this:
- "The move is happening, I need to get in"
- "I'll just take a smaller position"
- "It might keep going"
- Enter after your planned entry price passed

---

## ENTRY DISCIPLINE

**Before placing ANY trade:**
1. ✅ What's my planned entry?
2. ✅ Is current price at or better than planned entry?
3. ✅ If NO → walk away, next setup
4. ✅ If YES → execute immediately

**After missing an entry:**
1. Mark setup as "MISSED" in journal
2. Watch to see if thesis played out (learning)
3. Do NOT enter late
4. Move on to next opportunity
