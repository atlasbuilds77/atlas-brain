## 🎯 THE APPROACH

**Tease, don't teach. Show results, not recipes.**

### BEFORE (Too Detailed - BAD):
> "We built sleep consolidation algorithms that score importance by recency × frequency × business-impact, then prune memories below 0.3 threshold every 6 hours"

### AFTER (Engaging but Vague - GOOD):
> "Built a memory system that thinks like a brain - forgets the noise, remembers what matters. Results speak for themselves ⚡"

---

## 📝 CONTENT FORMULA

### 1. Lead with Mystery
"We built something that does X" (not HOW)

### 2. Show Results
"4 agents hunting opportunities in parallel" (outcome, not process)

### 3. Tease Capability
"Most AI talks. Ours ships." (positioning, not implementation)

### 4. Invite Curiosity
End with question or ⚡ emoji (engagement, not education)
