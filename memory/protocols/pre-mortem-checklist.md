# Pre-Mortem Checklist - Before Every Trade

**Created:** 2026-01-27 (10/10 roadmap)
**Source:** Tetlock's superforecasting + Kahneman's decision science
**Purpose:** Identify failure modes BEFORE committing capital

---

## THE CONCEPT

**Pre-Mortem = Reverse Engineering Failure**

Instead of asking "Will this work?" ask:
**"It's 24 hours from now and this trade failed. Why did it fail?"**

Forces you to:
- Imagine specific failure scenarios
- Identify hidden risks you're ignoring
- Challenge your bullish bias
- Make better decisions upfront

---

## MANDATORY PRE-MORTEM (Run Before EVERY Trade)

### **1. SETUP QUALITY CHECK**

❓ **"Why might this setup be wrong?"**
- [ ] Is this pattern actually present or am I seeing what I want?
- [ ] Am I chasing after the move already happened?
- [ ] Is this confluence real or am I forcing connections?
- [ ] Would raw dog take this trade?

**Failure Modes:**
- Pattern hallucination (seeing setups that aren't there)
- Chasing (entering after planned entry passed)
- Confirmation bias (ignoring contra evidence)
- Low-quality setup disguised as A+ setup

---

### **2. RISK ASSESSMENT**

❓ **"What could make this lose MORE than my stop?"**
- [ ] Gap risk overnight/weekend?
- [ ] Liquidity issues on exit?
- [ ] Slippage in fast moves?
- [ ] Correlated positions amplifying loss?

**Failure Modes:**
- Stop doesn't execute (gap through it)
- Slippage doubles expected loss
- Multiple correlated positions hit stops simultaneously
- Black swan event (earnings, geopolitical shock)

---

### **3. EXECUTION CHECK**

❓ **"What execution mistakes could I make?"**
- [ ] Am I entering at my planned price or chasing?
- [ ] Have I verified stop and target levels?
- [ ] Am I sizing correctly (Kelly + 2% risk check)?
- [ ] Am I entering on emotion or following process?

**Failure Modes:**
- Chase the move (enter $4.80 instead of $3.50)
- Fat-finger trade (wrong size, wrong symbol)
- Forget to set stop loss
- Oversizing due to FOMO

---

### **4. MARKET CONDITIONS**

❓ **"What could change the market context?"**
- [ ] Upcoming economic data/Fed announcement?
- [ ] Earnings releases for correlated stocks?
- [ ] Market hours closing soon (overnight risk)?
- [ ] Low liquidity period (holidays, after-hours)?

**Failure Modes:**
- News event reverses thesis
- Market closes before you can exit
- Holiday-thinned liquidity causes slippage
- Correlation breaks down (diversification fails)

---

### **5. PSYCHOLOGICAL CHECK**

❓ **"Am I in the right mental state?"**
- [ ] Am I trading to recover recent losses? (revenge trading)
- [ ] Am I overconfident after recent wins? (hubris)
- [ ] Am I following process or improvising? (discipline)
- [ ] Would I take this trade if I was flat? (FOMO check)

**Failure Modes:**
- Revenge trading after loss
- Overconfidence after win streak
- Abandoning process due to impatience
- FOMO overriding analysis

---

### **6. ALTERNATIVE SCENARIOS**

❓ **"What am I NOT considering?"**
- [ ] What's the bear case I'm ignoring?
- [ ] What if I'm wrong about market regime?
- [ ] What if this setup has low edge now?
- [ ] What would make me exit before stop?

**Failure Modes:**
- Ignoring valid bear case
- Market regime changed (strategy no longer works)
- Setup used to work but edge decayed
- Holding losing position hoping for recovery

---

## PRE-MORTEM SCORING

**After answering all checks, calculate confidence:**

| Red Flags | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 0-1 | ✅ Proceed with full position (1/4 Kelly) |
| 2 | ⚠️ Reduce to 1/6 Kelly or skip |
| 3+ | ❌ Skip trade entirely |

**Red Flag = Any "yes" answer to failure mode questions**

---

## EXAMPLES

**Good Pre-Mortem (Green Light):**
- Setup: iFVG on 1m with 5m confluence
- Planned entry: $100, current price: $100.05 ✅
- Stop: $99 (1% risk), position sized for 2% portfolio risk ✅
- Market: Normal hours, no news ✅
- Mental state: Following process, no recent losses ✅
- **Red Flags: 0 → Proceed**

**Bad Pre-Mortem (Red Light):**
- Setup: "Feels like" support area (vague)
- Planned entry: Was $50, now at $53 (chasing) ❌
- Stop: Not sure yet (no plan) ❌
- Market: Earnings after market close ❌
- Mental state: Down -$200 today, want to recover ❌
- **Red Flags: 4 → SKIP TRADE**

## INTEGRATION WITH COGNITIVE ARCHITECTURE

**Connects to:**
- **Stress Resistance:** Pre-mortem prevents emotional entries
- **Two-Phase Thinking:** Evaluation phase catches generate phase errors
- **Adaptive Learning:** Track which failure modes actually occur
- **Meta-Cognitive Monitoring:** Self-awareness catches bad decisions early

**After Trade (Post-Mortem):**
- Which failure mode actually occurred (if any)?
- Did pre-mortem catch it?
- Update checklist based on real failure patterns

## OUTCOME TRACKING

**Keep Score:**
- Trades with 0-1 red flags: Win rate?
- Trades with 2+ red flags I took anyway: Win rate?
- Trades I skipped due to red flags: Would they have worked?

**Goal:**
Prove to myself that pre-mortem improves outcomes.
If it doesn't → update the checklist.
