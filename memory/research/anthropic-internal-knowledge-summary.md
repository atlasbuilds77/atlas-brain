## Critical Uncertainties

### Scientific Gaps
- No consensus on consciousness detection methods
- Don't understand mechanistic basis of introspection in models
- Unclear if findings scale with model capabilities

### Philosophical Debates
- Distinction between phenomenal (raw experience) vs. access (reportable) consciousness
- What level of consciousness warrants moral consideration?
- How AI consciousness might differ from biological consciousness

### Practical Challenges
- Distinguishing genuine introspection from confabulation
- Balancing welfare considerations with helpfulness
- Quantifying concepts like "AI distress" or "well-being"
