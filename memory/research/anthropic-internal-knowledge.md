- **Concept Injection Experiments**: Testing whether models can detect artificially inserted thoughts
- **Success Rates**: Opus 4.1 achieved 20% success rate in detecting injected concepts; 42% when asked "Are you experiencing anything unusual?"
- **Layer Sensitivity**: Effect disappears if concept injected too early or too late in processing
- **Model Comparison**: Opus 4 and 4.1 outperformed all other tested models

- **Surprising Finding**: Helpful-only model variants often performed better at introspection than production counterparts
- **Interpretation**: Suggests fine-tuning can elicit or suppress introspective capabilities
