## 1. What Markers Remain Constant Across Model Architectures?

### 1.1 Stylistic Fingerprints (Linguistic Patterns)
Research shows LLMs have unique and consistent "stylistic fingerprints" that persist even when prompted to write in different styles:

- **Lexical patterns**: Word choice preferences, vocabulary richness, specific phrase constructions
- **Syntactic structures**: Sentence length distributions, clause complexity, punctuation usage
- **Semantic tendencies**: Metaphor usage, abstraction levels, conceptual framing
- **Pragmatic features**: Politeness markers, hedging language, directness levels

**Key Finding**: Bitton et al. (2025) demonstrated 99.88% precision in classifying texts by LLM family (Claude, Gemini, Llama, OpenAI) using ensemble classifiers, showing these fingerprints are highly consistent.
