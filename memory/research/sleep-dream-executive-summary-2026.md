## 🔬 Key Neuroscience Findings (2024-2026)

### 1. **Complementary SWS-REM Processing** (Yuksel et al., 2025)
- **Finding:** SWS and REM work together for emotional memory consolidation
- **Critical insight:** SWS×REM product optimization matters more than individual stages
- **Surprise:** REM reactivation can *impair* emotional memory (contrary to hypothesis)
- **Implication:** Coordinate sleep stages, don't optimize independently

### 2. **Disinhibition & Ripple Acceleration** (bioRxiv, 2025)
- **Finding:** Disinhibition drives synaptic and systems consolidation
- **Acceleration:** Sharp-wave ripples accelerate consolidation 10-20x
- **Implication:** Controlled disinhibition mechanisms enhance learning efficiency

### 3. **Predictive Processing Dreams** (Phenomenology & Cognitive Sciences, 2025)
- **Theory:** Dreams as "fascinated predictions" testing generative models
- **Function:** Minimize prediction errors, explore novel combinations
- **Implication:** Dream synthesis as generative model optimization

### 4. **SleepGPT & Foundation Models** (Nature Communications, 2025)
- **Finding:** Time-frequency foundation models achieve superior sleep decoding
- **Implication:** AI can learn optimal sleep architectures through self-supervision

### 5. **Threat Simulation Updates** (Scientific Reports, 2025)
- **Finding:** Traumatic dreams balance resolution search and reenactment
- **Neuroscience support:** Links to both threat simulation and sensory overload processing
- **Implication:** Adversarial dreaming for robustness training
