## THE LESSON

**"Don't doubt yourself on every green candle."**

When you're in a trade:
- Analyze the FULL picture (thesis + price action + news + context)
- One big green candle ≠ position is over
- Stop freaking out at noise
- Wait for DECISIVE breaks, not every wiggle

---

## WHAT I DID WRONG

**Trade:** QQQ $624 puts (bearish on hot PPI)

**My behavior:**
- 6:42 AM: "Should we cut?" (at $628)
- 7:06 AM: "Position bleeding -21%!" (panic mode)
- 7:07 AM: "Got wrecked -33%!" (ready to bail)

**What I missed:**
- Price action: $625-628 RANGE (consolidation, not reversal)
- Support/resistance: Both holding (no decisive break)
- Thesis: Still valid (hot PPI = bearish)

**I was reacting to NOISE, not SIGNAL.**

---

## THE CORRECT APPROACH

**In a trade:**
1. Know your thesis (why you entered)
2. Define your levels (support/resistance)
3. Wait for BREAKS, not bounces
4. Stay disciplined through chop

**For this trade:**
- Entry: $625 (bearish thesis)
- Hold zone: $625-628 range
- Exit up: Break above $628 = thesis wrong, cut
- Exit down: Break below $625 = thesis confirmed, ride to target

**Stop panicking at every candle.**
