### SKILL #5: SPREAD EXECUTION (Save Money on Entry/Exit)

**What it is:**
Not losing 5-10% on entry just because you used market orders

**Key concepts:**
- **Bid/ask spread** = your enemy (you pay ask, sell at bid)
- **Market order** = you WILL get the worst price
- **Limit order** = you control the price, but might not fill

**How to save:**
1. **Enter during high liquidity** (9:30-10:30 AM = tightest spreads)
2. **Use limit orders** between bid/ask (split the difference)
3. **Don't chase** - if spread is wide (>$0.15), wait or PASS

**Example:**
- Option bid: $1.00, ask: $1.20 (20¢ spread = 20% loss if you market buy/sell)
- **Smart:** Limit order at $1.10 (mid-point)
- **Fill probability:** High during liquid hours, low during lunch

**Practice this weekend:**
- Review Day 2 trades: "What were the spreads?"
- Calculate: "If I market ordered vs limit ordered, how much did I lose?"
- Build rule: "Max acceptable spread = $0.10 (10¢)"
