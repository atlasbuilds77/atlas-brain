## 🎯 How to Use This Framework

### **First Time Setup (1-2 hours):**
1. Read **psychology-fundamentals.md** cover to cover
2. Read **common-trader-mistakes.md** (learn from pain)
3. Print **discipline-checklist.md** (keep next to your screen)
4. Skim **emotional-management.md** (know where techniques are)

### **Daily Routine:**

**Pre-Market (5 minutes):**
- Review discipline-checklist.md Part 1 (Mental State)
- Set intention for the day
- Define A+ setups you'll trade

**Per Trade (2-3 minutes):**
- Work through discipline-checklist.md Parts 2-4
- If ANY box is unchecked → DON'T TRADE
- Execute only if ALL criteria met

**After Losses:**
- Close platform immediately
- 10-minute break minimum
- Reference emotional-management.md (Revenge section)
- Journal before returning

**After Wins:**
- Don't get euphoric
- Reference emotional-management.md (Euphoria section)
- Consider trading smaller tomorrow

**End of Day (10 minutes):**
- Complete discipline-checklist.md Part 7 (Session Review)
- Journal all trades
- Rate emotional control
- Plan tomorrow

### **Weekly Review (30 minutes):**
- Review all journals from the week
- Identify emotional patterns
- Check: Are you making same mistakes?
- Reference common-trader-mistakes.md
- Adjust rules as needed

### **Monthly Deep Dive (1 hour):**
- Big picture emotional trends
- Process quality over 100+ trades
- Re-read psychology-fundamentals.md sections that need reinforcement
- Update discipline checklist based on patterns

## 🧠 Core Truths to Remember

1. **"Trading is 80% psychology, 20% strategy"**
   - Most traders have it backwards

2. **"Your worst enemy isn't the market - it's you"**
   - Master yourself → Master trading

3. **"Process over outcome"**
   - Good trade can lose; bad trade can win

4. **"You control the inputs, not the outputs"**
   - Control: entry, size, stop, exit plan
   - Don't control: market direction, trade outcome

5. **"Emotional traders donate to disciplined traders"**
   - Be the disciplined one

6. **"The best trade is the one you don't take"**
   - Patience is an edge

7. **"Risk management is your #1 job"**
   - You're a risk manager who occasionally gets paid

8. **"The market doesn't owe you anything"**
   - No revenge; accept uncertainty

9. **"If in doubt, sit it out"**
   - The ultimate rule

10. **"This checklist is your shield against yourself"**
    - Use it every time
