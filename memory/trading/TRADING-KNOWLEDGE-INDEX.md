## 🚨 CRITICAL RULES (NEVER BREAK)

### Risk Management:
- **Max risk per trade:** 1-2% of account
- **Session loss limit:** -$50 → size down to $25
- **Daily loss limit:** -$100 → STOP trading
- **Position sizing:** Max 33% of account per trade

### Trading Discipline:
- **Only trade Kill Zones** (London/NY)
- **Wait for A+ setups** (liquidity sweep + Order Block + SMT)
- **Never move stops** (pre-defined or don't trade)
- **Never revenge trade** (10-minute cooldown after losses)
- **Never chase** (buy discount, sell premium only)

### Psychology:
- **Check emotional state** before every trade (Traffic Light)
- **If Yellow/Red** → Don't trade (wait for Green)
- **After losses** → 10-minute break minimum
- **If tilting** → Close platform, journal, walk away
