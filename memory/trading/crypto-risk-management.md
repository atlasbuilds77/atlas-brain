# Crypto Risk Management for Small Portfolios ($100-200 Range)

*Research conducted on January 25, 2026*

## Executive Summary

This document outlines risk management strategies for small crypto portfolios in the $100-200 range, focusing on Solana DEX trading. Small accounts face unique challenges including limited capital for diversification, higher relative fees, and vulnerability to scams. Proper risk management is essential for survival and growth.

## 1. Position Sizing Strategies for Small Accounts

### Core Principles for Small Capital:
- **Micro-Position Sizing**: With $100-200, allocate 5-10% ($5-20) per trade
- **Risk Per Trade**: Never risk more than 1-2% of total capital per trade ($1-4)
- **Concentration vs Diversification**: 
  - Small accounts benefit from focused positions (2-3 assets max)
  - Avoid over-diversification which dilutes potential gains
  - Consider 70% core positions, 20% rotations, 10% degen plays

### Practical Approaches:
1. **Fixed Dollar Amount**: Allocate fixed $ amounts per trade (e.g., $15 per position)
2. **Percentage-Based**: 5-10% of portfolio per trade
3. **Pyramid Scaling**: Start with small position, add on confirmation
4. **Lottery Ticket Approach**: Allocate 5-10% to high-risk/high-reward plays

### Key Insights from Research:
- "Small capital + big dreams + zero process = guaranteed frustration" - Trading expert
- Many successful traders start with $80-100 wallets and grind them up
- Focus on percentage gains rather than dollar amounts
- "You don't NEED a lot of capital to make money" - Experienced trader

## 2. Stop-Loss Strategies on Solana DEXs

### Challenges on Solana DEXs:
- **400ms block time** can cause execution delays
- **Mempool congestion** during high volatility
- **Slippage** on low-liquidity tokens
- **No native stop-loss** on most DEXs (requires manual monitoring)

### Practical Stop-Loss Solutions:

#### Technical Stop-Loss Levels:
- **Percentage-Based**: 10-15% for established tokens, 20-30% for memes
- **Support/Resistance**: Set stops below key support levels
- **ATR-Based**: 1.5-2x Average True Range from entry
- **Moving Averages**: Stop below key EMAs (7, 25, 99)

#### Execution Strategies:
1. **Mental Stops**: Pre-defined exit points (requires discipline)
2. **Limit Orders**: Set sell orders at stop levels in advance
3. **Automated Tools**: Use trading bots or platforms with stop-loss features
4. **Time-Based Exits**: Exit if trade doesn't work within timeframe

#### Key Considerations:
- "Stop-Loss = Survival. No trade is worth your account." - Risk management expert
- Account for Solana's 400ms block time when setting stops
- Use wider stops during high volatility periods
- Consider transaction fees when setting stop levels

## 3. How to Avoid Rug Pulls and Scams

### Red Flags to Watch For:

#### Token Contract Analysis:
1. **Renounced Ownership**: Check if contract ownership is renounced
2. **Liquidity Lock**: Verify liquidity is locked (and for how long)
3. **Mint Function**: Avoid tokens with active mint functions
4. **Tax Rates**: Be wary of high buy/sell taxes (>10%)
5. **Holder Distribution**: Check for whale concentration (>5% holders)

#### Project Fundamentals:
1. **Anonymous Teams**: Higher risk with anonymous developers
2. **Copy-Paste Websites**: Generic templates with little original content
3. **Guaranteed Returns**: Any promise of guaranteed profits is a scam
4. **Rushed Launches**: Pressure to buy immediately
5. **No Roadmap/Whitepaper**: Lack of clear project direction

#### Community & Social Signals:
1. **Bot Activity**: Artificial engagement on social media
2. **Paid Shillers**: Obvious paid promotion without substance
3. **No Doxxed Team**: Legitimate projects usually have public teams
4. **Suspicious TG Groups**: Admin deleting messages, banning questions

### Protective Measures:
1. **Use Analysis Tools**: Platforms like Axiom Trading Platform for token analysis
2. **Start Small**: Test with minimal amounts first
3. **Verify Everything**: Cross-check contract addresses, websites, socials
4. **Avoid FOMO**: Don't chase pumps without due diligence
5. **Stick to Established**: Prefer tokens with track record and community

### Key Warning Signs from Research:
- "Never buy rug pull tokens" - Multiple scam alerts
- "Slow rugs are the most dangerous" - Chain Alpha
- Multiple reports of fake TG groups charging fees then disappearing
- Tools like Axiom recommended for comprehensive token analysis

- **Percentage Mindset**: Think in % gains, not $ amounts

- **Continuous Learning**: Journal, review, improve
