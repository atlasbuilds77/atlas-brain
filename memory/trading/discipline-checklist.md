# Pre-Trade Discipline Checklist

> "The goal of a successful trader is to make the best trades. Money is secondary." - Alexander Elder

**Purpose:** Use this checklist BEFORE every trade to ensure you're trading like a professional, not gambling.

**Rule:** If you can't check EVERY box → DON'T TAKE THE TRADE

---

## Part 1: Mental State Check (Pre-Session)

**Complete this BEFORE opening your trading platform**

### Emotional State
- [ ] I am emotionally neutral (not euphoric, angry, desperate, or anxious)
- [ ] I am well-rested (6+ hours sleep)
- [ ] I am not trading to "make back" previous losses
- [ ] I am not trading to "prove" something to myself or others
- [ ] I am not distracted by personal issues or stress
- [ ] I have no urge to "revenge trade" from yesterday

**If ANY box is unchecked → Take the day off. The market will be here tomorrow.**

### Physical State
- [ ] I am mentally alert and focused
- [ ] I have eaten and am hydrated
- [ ] I am not under influence of substances (alcohol, excessive caffeine, etc.)
- [ ] I have no time pressure (meetings, appointments that will interrupt)

### System State
- [ ] I have reviewed my trading plan
- [ ] I have reviewed yesterday's journal (if applicable)
- [ ] I know my max position size for today
- [ ] I know my daily loss limit (2-3% max)
- [ ] I have my A+ setups defined and written down

**Mental Affirmations (Say Out Loud):**
1. "I am a risk manager, not a profit chaser"
2. "I only trade MY setups"
3. "I follow my rules without exception"
4. "Process over outcome"
5. "This single trade means nothing; my system means everything"

---

## Part 2: Setup Validation (Pre-Trade)

**Complete for EACH individual trade before entering**

### Is This My Setup?
- [ ] This matches one of my pre-defined A+ setups
- [ ] I planned to look for this setup today (not random)
- [ ] This is NOT FOMO (I didn't just see someone else trade it)
- [ ] This is NOT revenge trading (not trying to make back losses)
- [ ] This is NOT boredom (not trading just for action)

**If this is NOT your setup → Skip it. Wait for YOUR setup.**

### Technical Validation
- [ ] My entry point is clearly defined
- [ ] My stop loss level is clearly defined
- [ ] My profit target is clearly defined
- [ ] Risk/Reward ratio is AT LEAST 1.5:1 (ideally 2:1+)
- [ ] I have confluence (multiple reasons for this trade)
- [ ] The setup is still valid NOW (not 10 minutes ago)

### Risk Management
- [ ] Position size calculated: $_______ per share/contract
- [ ] Total risk = ___% of account (must be ≤ 1-2%)
- [ ] Stop loss = $_______ per share/contract
- [ ] Stop loss placement makes technical sense (not arbitrary)
- [ ] I can afford to lose this full amount and be okay
- [ ] Total risk in $ = $_______ (max $_______ based on account)

**Position Size Formula:**
```
Position Size = (Account Risk %) / (Entry - Stop Loss)

Example:
Account = $10,000
Risk = 1% = $100
Entry = $50
Stop = $49
Position Size = $100 / ($50 - $49) = 100 shares
```

### Time Check
- [ ] I am not rushing this decision
- [ ] I waited at least 30 seconds after seeing this setup
- [ ] If feeling FOMO, I waited 5 minutes (setup still valid?)
- [ ] This is not the last 10 minutes of market (emotional close)
- [ ] I have time to manage this trade properly

## Part 3: Trade Execution Check

**Complete BEFORE clicking the order button**

### Pre-Entry Final Check
- [ ] Entry order type selected: _____ (market/limit)
- [ ] Position size entered: _____ shares/contracts
- [ ] Stop loss order ready to place: YES / NO
- [ ] Profit target order ready to place: YES / NO
- [ ] I know exactly what I'll do if price moves against me
- [ ] I know exactly what I'll do if price moves for me

### The Five Questions
**Answer these honestly:**

1. **"What is my thesis for this trade?"**
   - Answer: _______________________________________________

2. **"Where am I wrong?"**
   - Answer (stop loss level): _______________________________________________

3. **"Where am I right?"**
   - Answer (profit target): _______________________________________________

4. **"Why am I taking this trade NOW?"**
   - Answer: _______________________________________________

5. **"Am I okay losing my full risk on this trade?"**
   - Answer: YES / NO

**If you can't answer all 5 clearly → Don't trade**

### Emotional Red Flags
**If ANY of these are true → DON'T TRADE:**
- [ ] I "need" this trade to work
- [ ] I'm thinking about how much money I'll make
- [ ] I'm feeling desperate or anxious
- [ ] I'm worried about what others will think
- [ ] I'm hoping or praying this works
- [ ] I feel like I "have to" get in right now
- [ ] I'm ignoring any part of my checklist

**Green Light Emotions:**
- [ ] I'm calm and neutral
- [ ] I'm following my process
- [ ] I accept that this trade might lose
- [ ] I'm focused on execution, not outcome

## Part 5: Mid-Trade Discipline Check

**Use this if you feel tempted to deviate from your plan**

### Stop Loss Temptation Check
**If tempted to move stop loss:**
- [ ] STOP. Close chart for 30 seconds.
- [ ] Is price near my stop? YES / NO
- [ ] Am I hoping it will bounce? YES / NO
- [ ] Am I afraid of being wrong? YES / NO

**If YES to any → Your stop loss is correct. DO NOT MOVE IT.**

**Professional Response:**
- Keep stop where it is
- Accept that you might be stopped out
- Trust your original analysis
- If stopped out = proper risk management

### Exit Temptation Check
**If tempted to exit before stop/target:**
- [ ] Why do I want to exit?
  - Fear? → Stay in; trust your plan
  - Profit target hit? → Exit per plan
  - Setup invalidated? → Exit immediately
  - Boredom? → Stay in; trust your plan

- [ ] Is this an emotional reaction or rule-based decision?
  - Emotional → Don't act
  - Rule-based → Act immediately

### Add-On Temptation Check
**If tempted to add to position:**
- [ ] Was this planned BEFORE entry? YES / NO
- [ ] Is this because position is winning? (dangerous)
- [ ] Is this because I want to "maximize" the move? (greed)
- [ ] Will this exceed my max risk per trade?

**If adding wasn't pre-planned → DON'T ADD**

**Rate your trade execution (1-10):**

1. **Setup Quality**: ___/10
   - Was this truly an A+ setup?

2. **Entry Execution**: ___/10
   - Did I enter at my planned level?

3. **Risk Management**: ___/10
   - Was position size correct?
   - Was stop placement correct?

4. **Exit Execution**: ___/10

   - Did I move stops or panic exit?

5. **Emotional Control**: ___/10
   - Was I calm and disciplined?
   - Did emotions affect decisions?

**Overall Process Score: ___/50**

- 40-50: Excellent execution
- 30-39: Good execution with room for improvement
- 20-29: Poor execution; review mistakes
- 0-19: Terrible execution; take break before next trade

## Emergency Stop Protocol

**Activate immediately if:**
- 2 losses in a row
- 3% daily loss reached
- Feeling strong negative emotions
- Broke a major rule
- Revenge trading urge

**Protocol:**
1. Close all positions immediately
2. Close trading platform
3. Walk away for minimum 30 minutes
4. Journal what happened
5. Do NOT return until emotionally neutral
6. Review this checklist before re-entering
