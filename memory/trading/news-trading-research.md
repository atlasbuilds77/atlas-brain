# News-Based Trading Edge Research

## Fastest News Sources for Prediction Markets
- **Polymarket & Kalshi**: Dominant platforms where news moves markets fastest (NYTimes, Forbes).
- **Major Networks (CNN, CNBC)**: Impact markets via real-time coverage (Investopedia).

## Algorithmic Parsing & Reaction
- **NLP Tools**: Critical for parsing news/social media in real-time (Magmio).
- **Overreaction Patterns**: Algorithms trigger short-term spikes that reverse within minutes (Federal Reserve).
- **Social Media Advantage**: Twitter/Reddit often break news faster than traditional outlets (MoldStud).

## Sentiment Analysis Strategies
- **Real-Time Integration**: Enhances trade timing (Moody’s).
- **Hybrid Models**: Combining NLP (e.g., FinBERT) with technical indicators improves accuracy (arXiv).
- **Risks**: Public sentiment data can be gamed (e.g., fake news) (Reddit).

## Documented Profit Cases
- **Insider Trading**: Examples include Martoma ($70M profit) and Amazon leak ($115K profit).
- **Academic Backing**: News-based strategies outperform when paired with momentum (ScienceDirect).

## Key Takeaways
1. **Speed Focus**: Prioritize NLP tools and social media for sub-minute reactions.
2. **Hybrid Signals**: Merge sentiment with quant metrics to avoid overreaction traps.
3. **Ethical Boundaries**: Avoid gray-area strategies despite their profitability.
