# ICT Pre-Trade Quick Reference

## 🎯 Use this checklist BEFORE every trade

---

## THE A+ SETUP (All Must Align)

### ✅ 1. TIME CHECK
**Are we in a Kill Zone?**
- ❌ Asian (8PM-12AM) - Don't trade
- ⭐ London Open (2-5AM NY) - Good
- ⭐⭐⭐ NY AM (7-10AM NY) - BEST
- ⭐ London Close (10AM-12PM NY) - Good
- ❌ Lunch (12-1PM) - Don't trade
- ❌ Late session (4PM+) - Don't trade

**→ If not in Kill Zone, SKIP THE TRADE**

---

### ✅ 2. MARKET STRUCTURE
**What's the higher timeframe bias?**
- Daily chart: Bullish or Bearish?
- 4H chart: Confirming or diverging?

**Bullish Structure:**
- Higher Highs (HH) + Higher Lows (HL)
- → ONLY look for LONGS

**Bearish Structure:**
- Lower Highs (LH) + Lower Lows (LL)
- → ONLY look for SHORTS

**→ Never trade against daily bias**

---

### ✅ 3. LIQUIDITY
**Where are the stops?**

**Mark on chart:**
- Equal Highs (Buy-Side Liquidity - BSL)
- Equal Lows (Sell-Side Liquidity - SSL)
- Previous Day High/Low
- Round numbers (1.1000, 4000, etc.)

**Has liquidity been swept?**
- ❌ NO → WAIT (don't enter early)
- ✅ YES → Proceed to next step

**In uptrend:** Wait for SSL sweep (below lows)
**In downtrend:** Wait for BSL sweep (above highs)

**→ No liquidity sweep = No trade**

---

### ✅ 4. ORDER BLOCK
**Where did institutions enter?**

**After liquidity sweep:**
- Find the candle where reversal started
- That's your Order Block
- Drop to 1M chart to refine exact zone

**Bullish OB:** Last bearish candle before bullish move
**Bearish OB:** Last bullish candle before bearish move

**Must have:**
- Complete engulfment
- Imbalance (FVG) on lower timeframe
- Structure shift on 1M/5M

**→ No clear OB = No trade**

---

### ✅ 5. PREMIUM/DISCOUNT
**Is price in the right zone?**

**Draw Fibonacci from swing high to low:**
- 0-50% = Premium (expensive)
- 50-100% = Discount (cheap)

**In UPTREND (buy setups):**
- ✅ Discount zone (62-79%) = Optimal Trade Entry (OTE)
- ✅ Below 50% = Good
- ❌ Premium zone (above 50%) = DON'T BUY

**In DOWNTREND (sell setups):**
- ✅ Premium zone (21-38%) = OTE
- ✅ Above 50% = Good
- ❌ Discount zone (below 50%) = DON'T SELL

**→ Wrong zone = Skip the trade**

---

### ✅ 6. CONFIRMATION (Optional but Recommended)
**Additional confluence:**

**SMT Divergence:**
- Compare with correlated pair (EUR/GBP, ES/NQ)
- One makes new high/low, other doesn't?
- ✅ Trade the weaker pair

**Fair Value Gap (FVG):**
- 3-candle pattern with gap
- Price returning to fill it?
- ✅ Combine with Order Block

**Lower Timeframe Confirmation:**
- Drop to 1M chart
- Wait for structure shift (BOS or CHoCH)
- Rejection candle (pin bar, engulfing)
- ✅ Enter after confirmation

---

## 📊 ENTRY EXECUTION

### Entry Point
- Price tests Order Block
- 1M confirmation candle closes
- Enter market or limit order

### Stop Loss
- **Longs:** 10-20 pips below OB low
- **Shorts:** 10-20 pips above OB high
- If swept low/high → stop just beyond it

### Take Profit
- **Target 1:** 50% of range (1:1 or 1:2 RR)
- **Target 2:** Opposing liquidity pool
- **Target 3:** Previous swing high/low
- Scale out at each level

### Position Sizing
- Risk 1-2% of account per trade
- ICT setups have tight stops → no need for huge leverage

## 🚨 RED FLAGS (Don't Trade If…)

❌ Outside Kill Zone
❌ Against higher timeframe bias
❌ No liquidity sweep
❌ Can't identify clear Order Block
❌ Buying premium in uptrend
❌ Selling discount in downtrend
❌ News event in 30 minutes
❌ Friday afternoon (unpredictable)
❌ Major holiday week (low volume)

**→ If ANY red flag present, SKIP THE TRADE**

- Institutional entry zones
