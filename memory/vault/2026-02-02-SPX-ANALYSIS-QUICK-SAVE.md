## 5 SOLUTIONS SENT TO HUNTER (iMessage)

**1. TREND FILTER**
- Check 200-period MA on 15min
- Only long calls if above MA (uptrend)
- Only long puts if below MA (downtrend)

**2. RSI OVERBOUGHT/OVERSOLD**
- Don't buy calls if RSI > 70 (overbought = top)
- Don't buy puts if RSI < 30 (oversold = bottom)

**3. REQUIRE PULLBACK**
- Wait for price to dip/spike, then bounce/reject
- Enter on RETEST, not breakout
- Don't chase momentum

**4. VOLUME CONFIRMATION**
- Require volume INCREASING in direction of trade
- Declining volume = weak move, skip

**5. MULTI-TIMEFRAME CONFLUENCE**
