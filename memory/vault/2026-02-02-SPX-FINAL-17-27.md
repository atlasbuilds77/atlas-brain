## PROBLEM SOLVED

**January 2026 SPX Issue:**
- 30% win rate
- Average loss: -$1,567 (-70 to -99%)
- 5 out of 7 losses went to zero
- Total loss: -$8,215 (81% of portfolio)

**Root cause:** Expensive contracts ($2k+) riding to zero

---

## SOLUTION IMPLEMENTED

### 1. Position Sizing (Commit 95e6471)
**DTE:**
- Old: 1-3 DTE
- New: 1 DTE only

**Premium:**
- Old: $400-$2,500 per contract
- New: $500-$1,000 per contract

**Strike Selection:**
- Old: Delta-based (0.30, close to ATM)
- New: $10-15 OTM offset from spot

**Files:**
- `Helios SPX ALGO.txt` - replaced with Zenith v54
- `main.py` - SPX config + strike selection logic

### 2. Profit Monitor (Commit aab2f79)
**Thresholds:** 10%, 20%, 30%, 40%, 50%, 60%, 70%, 80%

**Background Monitor:**
- Checks every 10 minutes
- Only during market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET)
- Weekdays only
- Auto-starts on server boot

**No Spam:**
- Silent when no SPX positions
- Silent when no thresholds crossed
- Only alerts once per threshold per position
- SPX-only (doesn't check IWM/QQQ)
