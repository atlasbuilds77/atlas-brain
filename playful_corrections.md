## Tone Guidelines
- Keep it light and playful, not critical
- Use emojis to soften the correction
- Match the user's energy level
- Short enough for quick iMessage replies
- Works for friends, family, and casual conversations

## Usage Tips
1. Pick based on how close/far the misspelling is
2. Match the user's tone (more/less playful)
3. Use simpler corrections for quick chats
4. Save the teasing for friends who appreciate it
5. Always include the correct spelling clearly
