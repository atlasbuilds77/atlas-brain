# Crypto Scalping Indicators - Research Summary

## Key Findings for Main Agent

### Optimal Indicator Settings for Perpetuals Scalping:

**1. RSI (Relative Strength Index)**
- **Best for scalping:** 7-9 period (vs standard 14)
- **Thresholds:** 80/20 or 75/25 for aggressive trading
- **Why:** Faster signals on 1-5 minute charts

**2. MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence)**
- **Linda Raschke settings:** 3-10-16 with Simple MAs
- **Alternative:** 8-17-9 with Exponential MAs  
- **Advantage:** Detects trends 5-10 candles earlier than standard 12-26-9

**3. Bollinger Bands**
- **Scalping optimized:** 10-14 period, 1.5-1.9 standard deviations
- **Standard:** 20 period, 2.0 standard deviations
- **Use:** Tighter bands for quick mean reversion trades

**4. Volume Profile & VWAP**
- **Focus:** Point of Control (POC), Value Area levels
- **VWAP:** Key for institutional flow identification
- **Best with:** 30min-4hr lookback for intraday trading

### Most Effective Combinations:

1. **RSI(7) + Bollinger Bands(10,1.9)** - Quick oversold/overbought signals
2. **MACD(3,10,16) + Volume Profile POC** - Early trend changes at key levels
3. **Multi-timeframe alignment** - Higher TF direction + lower TF entries

### Timeframe Recommendations:
- **Ultra-scalping:** 1-3 minute charts
- **Standard scalping:** 5-15 minute charts  
- **Swing scalping:** 15-30 minute charts

### Critical Risk Management:
- Max 1-2% risk per trade
- 0.5-1.5% stop losses
- Monitor perpetuals funding rates
- Avoid >10x leverage for consistency

- Cryptofutures.trading indicator guide
- Linda Raschke MACD settings (3-10-16 SMA)
- Professional trading forums and backtesting results
- Volume Profile strategies from TradingView community

**Bottom Line:** Successful scalping requires optimized fast-reacting indicators combined with volume confirmation and strict risk management. The 3-10-16 MACD with 7-period RSI and tight Bollinger Bands (10,1.9) appears most effective for quick perpetuals trades.
