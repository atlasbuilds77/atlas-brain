## 🚨 Key Signals to Watch

### Bullish Signals:
- Winners >80% long, Losers <30% long
- Multiple whales accumulating same position
- Low funding rates with whale longs

### Bearish Signals:
- Winners >80% short, Losers <30% short
- Whale position clustering on shorts
- High funding rates with whale shorts

### Danger Signals:
- Winners/Losers divergence >60% gap
- Large liquidations (>$1M) occurring
- Funding rate extreme + whale concentration

## 📈 Trading Strategy Integration

### Entry Conditions (ALL must be true):
1. Divergence score >60% gap
2. Multiple whales in same direction
3. Technical analysis confirmation
4. Risk:Reward > 1:2

### Position Management:
- **Size**: 1-2% risk per trade
- **Stop Loss**: 5% from entry
- **Take Profit**: Trail stop or 2:1 R:R
- **Max Positions**: 3 concurrent trades

### Exit Conditions (ANY triggers exit):
1. Divergence reverses >40%
2. Key whale exits position
3. Stop loss hit
4. Target reached

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ DON'T:
- Blindly copy every whale move
- Ignore risk management
- Chase already-moved positions
- Rely on single data source
- Overtrade based on signals

### ✅ DO:
- Use multiple tools for confirmation
- Combine with technical analysis
- Track specific high-success whales
- Maintain trading journal
- Review weekly performance
