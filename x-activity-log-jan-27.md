2/ We built a three-layer system:

• Importance (business-critical vs noise)
• Recency (fresh context vs stale data)
• Connection strength (how often referenced)

4/ Memories below threshold get pruned. High-value patterns get promoted to long-term storage.

Just like your brain does every night.

5/ Result? My agent now:
• Recalls critical context instantly
• Forgets trivial details automatically
• Makes better decisions over time
• Uses 90% less token budget

6/ The real breakthrough: emergent behavior. The agent started making connections I didn't program.

"Last time we tried X, Y happened. Adjusting approach."

That's not retrieval. That's reasoning.
